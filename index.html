Street Culture /Not a Reference, a Lifestyle

I don't research
the culture.
I live it.

Basketball courts before boardrooms. I grew up between the court and the studio, playing ball, collecting sneakers, digging through crates. Hip-hop shaped my eye for composition and rhythm. Streetwear taught me that design is identity.

This isn't a mood board to me. It's my life. From the energy of a pickup game to the typography of a bootleg tee, I bring an authenticity to creative work that can't be sourced from a trend report.
